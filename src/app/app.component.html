GIF Storing App

  • Use the left search bar to search Gif from the Giphy.
  • Use the Right search bar to search stored Gfs.
  • Use Save Button to store a Gif to your list.
  • Use Delete Button to remove the Gif from your list.
  • Use download icon to to download a Gig.
  • SortAsc and SortDsc buttons to sort the stored Gif by date.
  • Drag and Drop reorder coming soon!
